# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in HireMirror AI a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter any unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing **Codeburner99@gmail.com**. All reports will remain confidential.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action to remove, block, or ban users who violate this code of conduct.
